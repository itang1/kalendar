Kalendar

The Christian year, laid out day by day.

Or browse the calendar right here in your browser →

What it does

  • A year at a glance. Every day of the coming year as a tile, colored by its liturgical season, always starting with today.
  • Feasts and solemnities. Saints' days, movable feasts, and the great solemnities, each with a short explanation of what is being celebrated.
  • The wheel. See the whole shape of the year at once as colored slices.
  • Private notes. Keep a note on any day. Notes sync privately through your own iCloud account and persist from year to year.

Privacy

Kalendar collects no data of its own and contains no analytics or tracking. Notes you add sync only through your personal iCloud account, never through a server we control. Full details on the privacy policy page.
