Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Kalendar is designed to work entirely on your device. It is built so that your use of the app is nobody's business but your own.

What we collect

Nothing. Kalendar does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on any server. It makes no network requests and contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

Your notes

Any notes you add to a day are stored only on your device. They are not synced to us or to any other service, they do not appear on your other devices, and they are permanently deleted if you uninstall the app.

Third parties

Kalendar uses no third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks that collect data.

Children

Because the app collects no data at all, it is safe for users of any age.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
